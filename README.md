# Cadence
📝 To‑Do List App
A privacy‑first task manager with a clean design and powerful features — all running fully local on your device. No servers, no accounts, no email sign‑ups.

🔑 Highlights
Beautiful UI/UX with themes and click sounds

Encrypted Group Mode: share tasks securely or delegate them to members

Stats & Achievements to track progress

Offline & Local: your data never leaves your device

Zero Sign‑In: instant use, no personal info required

⚡ Whether you’re organizing solo or collaborating in groups, this app keeps things simple, secure, and stylish.
